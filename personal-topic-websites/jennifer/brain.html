  • Music can release chemicals in the brain like dopamine (which makes you feel happy) and endorphins (which reduce perception of pain).
  • It has also been proven that training in music changes the structure of the brain. Studies have compared non-musicians to musicians, and have found a significant increase of gray matter in motor and auditory related areas.
  • Treatment of diseases like dementia and Alzheimer's through music is also very common (it can trigger particular emotions, memories, and thoughts.)
